Hobbies

Drawing

Drawing is my passion. I've been exposed to art since I was a child, as virtually everyone in my family is artistic. Making drawings traditionally or digitally is therapeutic for me. It helps me relax, particularly during times of stress and pressure.

Table Tennis

I was exposed to table tennis when I was in fifth school. Since then, I've fallen in love with the sport. Table tennis is more than just a sport for me, it is my passion. Because of table tennis, I've met lifelong friends and created many happy memories that I will surely cherish for the rest of my life.

Layout

When I first started college, I knew nothing about layouting. However, after I was admitted into EVSO-OC's student publication office, I became acquainted with it. I started as a cartoonist, but today I'm also a layout artist. My goal with every layout is to create a layout that is both coherent and visually appealing.
